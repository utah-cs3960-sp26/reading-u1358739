There is one big thing that has to be their in order for autoresearch to work and that is a way for the AI to see if they made any progress.  If the AI doesn't have a way to check if what it got is good then it will go off on some random tangent that doesn't even make any scense.
I think a problem is suitable for this kind of research if and only if there is some "truth value" that the AI has access to.  Without some truth the AI won't get anywhere, and if the AI has the wrong truth it will get results based on that wrong truth.  With the example of world peace, the AI doesn't have access to a truth about what this even means (the world doesn't even have a defined truth to what this means).  So the AI can't decide if something it came up with is for or against that idea.
